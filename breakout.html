About

Breakout initially started as an internship project to understand how Godot engine works. Afterwards, I kept working and expanding the learnign posibilities of the project, by adding things that could be beneficial as a backend developer. Such things are the handling of the data (JSON), the server and the communication between the game engine and the server.

Project Info

  • Role: Gameplay Programmer
  • Team Size: 1
  • Time Frame: 4 Weeks
  • Engine: Godot (GDScript, Rust)
